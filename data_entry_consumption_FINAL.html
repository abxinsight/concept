AbXInsight Data Entry: Consumption

Screens 1 to 5. Users select products from the tenant catalogue and enter quantities. Product attributes are auto-filled from the catalogue.

Quickfox implementation guidance: Data entry searches the tenant catalogue using hospital code (mandatory in Hospital Module), and may also search by abx_product_id, name, and ATC. National codes may be searchable only if they exist in the tenant catalogue. If a user reuses the same hospital code, it represents additional consumption of the same product and must be captured via quantities, not new catalogue items. Catalogue setup and Excel seeding are defined in the separate Product Catalogue Management mockup.
